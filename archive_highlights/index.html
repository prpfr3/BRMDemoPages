Maps and Plans

We also have a vast collection of maps and plans covering much of the London Brighton and South Coast Railway and beyond. Here we see a plan of Wadhurst station drawn in 193X at a time when new drainage was being introduced. The original we hold in the archive is at a scale of 40 feet to one inch.

South Western Railway Magazine article

In the archive, we hold back copies of the South Western Railway Magazine for the years x to y. In this particular edition there is an article about the Woking orphanage for which our very own London Jack used to collect funding.
